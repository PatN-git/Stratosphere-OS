# Workflow Proposal: 5a_distribution-and-growth


This workflow is currently parked and is not wired into the live execution path. When activated, it serves to define the distribution and growth strategy for features/products at design/concept time.

---

## 1. First 10 Users
Define the plan to acquire the first 10 users:
- **Named Target Audience:** Identify 10 real people or one specific named place where the target audience gathers (not a generic demographic description).
- **First Concrete Move:** Post a helpful resource, direct-message them individually, or share a one-page waitlist.
- **Timing:** Start acquisition efforts before the build is completed.

---

## 2. Primary Acquisition Channel
Select exactly **one** primary acquisition channel (wedge) to focus on:
- **Outbound:** Direct outreach, cold emailing, or direct messages.
- **SEO/Content:** Producing high-value content targeting specific search terms.
- **Viral:** Build mechanisms where product usage naturally invites others.
- **Integration/Marketplace:** Leverage existing platforms or app stores.

---

## 3. Growth Loop Design
Design one specific growth loop shape from the three options:
1. **Content Loop:** Public output of product usage pulls in new external users.
2. **Invite Loop:** The core product flow requires inviting or collaborating with a second person.
3. **Signal Loop:** Visible usage of the product in public makes others want to copy/adopt it.

*Selection Question:* "How does one new user naturally bring in another?"
*Rule:* The enabling feature of the growth loop (e.g., shareable listing page, send-to-client step) belongs on the **V1** story list. A loop deferred to V2 is a loop that never starts spinning.

---

## 4. Single Cheap Loop Metric
Measure the health of the growth loop using a single, cheap metric:
- **Metric:** The percentage of new users acquired via another user's activity (referral share / viral factor).
